Bharatanatyam

Classical — Temple origin · Tamil Nadu

Famous practitioners of this dance style vary widely; you can list notable performers or choreographers here. Bharatanatyam is a classical Indian dance form known for intricate footwork, expressive hand gestures (mudras), and facial expressions.

  • Costume: Silk saree with pleated fan, temple jewelry, bells (ghungroo).
  • Signature: Nrtta (pure dance) with Abhinaya (expressive storytelling).
KT

Karagattam

Folk — Harvest/Temple · Tamil Nadu

Karagattam is a folk dance involving balancing a pot (karagam) on the head, often as a devotional offering to the rain goddess Mariamman. Includes acrobatics, rhythmic movements, and vibrant costumes.

  • Costume: Colorful sari or folk dress, tall pot decorated with flowers.
  • Signature: Balance and rhythmic head-spinning while keeping the karagam steady.
KL

Kolattam

Folk — Community · Tamil Nadu

Kolattam is a rhythmic folk stick dance where performers strike sticks together in choreographed patterns. It is social, upbeat, and often performed by both men and women in groups during harvest or weddings.

  • Costume: Simple traditional dress, sometimes with colorful sashes.
  • Signature: Pairwise stick rhythm patterns and circle formations.
PC

Purulia Chhau

Folk — Martial · West Bengal

Purulia Chhau is a masked martial folk dance from West Bengal combining storytelling, acrobatics, and martial arts movements. Often depicts stories from epics like Ramayana and Mahabharata.

  • Costume: Colorful masks, traditional costumes with swords and sticks.
  • Signature: Masked expressions with acrobatic martial movements.
RN

Rabindra Nritya

Lyrical — Tagore inspired · West Bengal

Lyrical dance inspired by Tagore’s poetry and music. Emphasizes grace, expressiveness, and emotive gestures. Performed in cultural programs and classical performances.

  • Costume: Simple elegant sarees for women, traditional dhoti-kurta for men.
  • Signature: Graceful movements synchronized with Rabindra Sangeet music.
GB

Gambhira

Folk — Satirical theatre · West Bengal

Gambhira is a folk theatre dance blending humor, satire, and storytelling. Features two performers narrating social messages, accompanied by traditional music and rhythmic steps.

  • Costume: Traditional dhoti-kurta and folk ornaments.
  • Signature: Satirical dialogues with rhythmic dance steps.
